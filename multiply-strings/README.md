# Multiply Strings

- **Difficulty:** Medium
- **Categories:** Math, String, Simulation
- **Time Complexity:** O(M×N)
- **Space Complexity:** O(M+N)

---

Given two non-negative integers as strings, return their product as a string. Do not use built-in big integer multiplication.

---

## Approach: Grade-School Multiplication

Simulate digit-by-digit multiplication. Product of digits at position i and j goes to positions i+j and i+j+1. Process carries after all multiplications.
